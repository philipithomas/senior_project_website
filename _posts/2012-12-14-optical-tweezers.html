--- layout: post title: Blood, Lasers, and Boltzmann - Optical Tweezers Paper ---

For the WUSTL biophysics laboratory, my lab partner and I just finished an experiment on optical tweezers. Specifically, we used an optical microscope with a laser to trap micrometer-sized beads and blood cells. In addition, we used the random walks of beads to calculate Boltzmann's Constant using the Stokes-Einstein Equation.
