--- layout: post title: Kill The Landline at Startup Weekend Columbia ---

I am at Startup Weekend Columbia, and at the launch yesterday I pitched an idea for untethering people from old phone numbers. 40 ideas were presented, and mine was picked as one of the top.

We are building Kill The Landline - a service to forward old numbers to new ones for a low price. Startup Weekend gives us 52 hours build a company, and tomorrow we plan to have a completed product and a working demonstration!

Learn more with our intro blog post, and keep up with our crazy 52 hours on Twitter, Facebook, and our blog!
