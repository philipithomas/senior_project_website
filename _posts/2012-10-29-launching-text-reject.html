--- layout: post title: Launching Text Reject ---

While waiting to pay at a Steak and Shake near campus last week, Andrew Hess and I observed a stranger approach our friend and ask for her phone number. Frazzled though obviously not flattered, she gave him her real phone number. Andrew and I knew that there had to be an easier way to handle unwanted suitors. We set out planning, began programming on Wednesday, and yesterday launched Text Reject in five cities.

Text Reject is entered into the WUSTL School of Engineering Discovery Competition, where it is the first product launched by the competition.
