--- layout: post title: Venture Draft Kicks Off ---

When Israel Vicars and I co-founded Automation Kit this summer, one of our early clients was the Brandon Williams Economic Development Corporation. They came to us aiming to create off-the-field opportunities for professional athletes. Israel and I provided creative direction and technical follow-through for their ideas.

One project they had been working on was a conference aiming to unite professional athletes and venture capitalists. They had already booked space for the conference at SLU, but it remained in early planning stages. Israel and I were able to contribute and help shape the whole conference. One of the most exciting parts was choosing the title, and we coined the name Venture Draft.

In just two weeks the conference kicks off, and seeing the name along with the early marketing material we created plastered throughout the media strikes me as mildly surreal. With Venture Draft popping up in business journals and speakers such as Daymond John of FUBU and Shark Tank fame scheduled for the weekend, it is rewarding to see what started as an idea become so real.

As a sponsor though Automation Kit, I look forward to attending Venture Draft in two weeks!
