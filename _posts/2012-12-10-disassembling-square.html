--- layout: post title: Disassembling the Square Credit Card Reader ---

Square changes the way individuals and small businesses accept credit card payments. They started this with a simple hardware credit card reader that plugs into the 3.5mm headphone jack on smartphones.

The design for the reader is patented, so the basic design of the reader is publicly available.

However, I still wanted to see what the circuitry and components of the reader looked like for myself. The pictures that follow are the disassembly of the Square Card Reader.
