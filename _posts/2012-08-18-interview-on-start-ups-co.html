Start-Ups.Co interviewed me about Food Trucks Near Me. Read the article here.

26. What advice do you have for fresh entrepreneurs? Ship the minimum, not the best. You can spend a year developing what you perceive to be the perfect problem, or you can get something minimum on the market in two months, then have ten great months to build on feedback, data, and motivational publicity to create a much better and more popular product in the same amount of time.
