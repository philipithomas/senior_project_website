--- layout: post title: Launching 'Food Trucks Near Me' ---

After the previous success of Food Truck STL, I update the platform so that it could be extended to multiple cities. I also set up a new LLC to run the food truck effort, Food Trucks Near Me LLC. So, today I launch Food Trucks Near Me: dashboards for local food trucks.

The press release is available here (PDF). The website launches into Chicago and my hometown Cleveland in addition to St. Louis.

I have incorporated much cool technology into the website. Specifically, Systematic Revenue integration has been fun, with Cultivation Capital having just invested in them.

If you have any feedback on the website, please put it on the Food Trucks Near Me Uservoice.

I have decided that, before expanding to many more cities, I need to do a full code overhaul to make the site faster. My project for the weekend is rendering the food truck tweets on the server rather than in the browser, and I plan on a major UI change too.

Stay tuned for more food truck updates!
