--- layout: post title: Paperless Resume ---

Paper resumes suck. They are inconvenient to carry around. They are boring to read. There are always multiple versions.

I decided to turn my resume into a website to address these concerns. This makes the site easy to access, and it allows linking to various projects. The site is based on the Spacelab Bootswatch, so design is responsive (try looking at it on your phone!). Storing the site as a public git repository allows version control and a degree of accountability as changes are made.
