The Problem

While looking to grab tacos for lunch from my favorite food truck, @TacoTruckSTL, I had trouble finding a good unified resource showing all of the St. Louis food trucks and where they were. After some google searches and poking around various twitter pages, I was able to get a feel for where various food trucks were in the city. I decided that a more unified display of the truck Twitter feeds was necessary.

Food Truck STL

Food Truck STL provides the mobile food dashboard for St. Louis. The website aggregates the twitter feeds for all of the local food trucks, color-codes tweets based on recency, allows easy access to menus, and more.

The responsive site design works on mobile phones, tablets, and computers.

The @FoodTruckSTL Twitter Feed and Food Truck STL Facebook Page update every 15 minutes to share the latest St. Louis food truck news.

Future

  • I'm populating the site with menus at the moment. If you have any updates, please email me.
  • I believe that the site is up-to-date, but if any trucks are missing, please email me!
  • I am working on improving the speed of the website - tweets currently render client-side, and I am working a server-side cache-based rendering that should improve the site.
